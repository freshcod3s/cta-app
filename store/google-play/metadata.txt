FULL_DESCRIPTION (max 4000):
Congress Trade Alerts surfaces every stock trade disclosed under the STOCK Act by members of the US House and Senate -- drawn from official public records, with no opinions, no spin, and no guesswork.

What you get
- Live feed of every congressional stock trade as it's disclosed
- Filter by individual member to follow specific Representatives or Senators
- Push alerts when a member you follow files a new disclosure
- Plain-language methodology so you know exactly where the data comes from and how it's processed
- Clear flags when a trade is filed late (the STOCK Act requires disclosure within 45 days; we surface that lag)

Who it's for
- Journalists tracking congressional compliance failures
- Civic-engaged citizens watching their representatives' financial activity
- Researchers studying public-record financial disclosure data

Data sources
- US House Clerk Periodic Transaction Reports (PTR)
- US Senate Office of Public Records (Senate EFD)
- Refreshed every 30 minutes from official filings

Privacy
- No user accounts required
- No analytics or third-party tracking
- Anonymous push tokens only -- we do not collect any personal data
- Open source: github.com/freshcod3s/cta-app

This app is not affiliated with the United States government or with any member of Congress. All data comes from public records that members of Congress are required by law to file under the Stop Trading on Congressional Knowledge Act of 2012.

Free. No ads. Updated daily.
