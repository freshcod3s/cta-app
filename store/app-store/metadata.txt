DESCRIPTION (max 4000):
Congress Trade Alerts surfaces stock trades disclosed under the STOCK Act by members of the US House and Senate -- drawn from public disclosure records, with no opinions and no ads.

FEATURES
- Feed of congressional stock trades, refreshed regularly as new filings are processed
- Push alerts for specific members and tickers, with minimum trade-size filter
- Trade detail with filing date, amount range, and market return comparison
- Conflict Score measuring overlap between committee assignments and sectors traded
- Related trades for the same ticker or by the same member
- Daily Dive with sector analysis, bipartisan trading patterns, and newly filing members
- Member profiles with full trading history, committee seats, party, state, and chamber
- Ticker pages showing all congressional trades for any stock
- Committee pages with rosters, subcommittees, hearings, markups, and votes
- Leaderboard ranking members by trading frequency and volume
- Local watchlist -- saved on your device, never uploaded
- Full methodology with data sources, update frequency, lag stats, and calculation details

DATA SOURCES
Trade data is aggregated from public STOCK Act disclosures via the third-party aggregator Capitol Trades, which compiles filings originating from the US House Office of the Clerk and the US Senate. Company and sector classification (GICS) is provided by Finnhub. Each trade links to its official source filing.

Individual records may contain errors, including a trade shown under the wrong member; verify against the official source linked on each trade.

WHO IT'S FOR
- Journalists tracking congressional compliance and conflicts of interest
- Civic-minded citizens watching their representatives' financial activity
- Researchers studying public financial-disclosure data

PRIVACY
No account required. No tracking. No ads. No third-party analytics. The only data stored is an anonymous push token if you enable notifications -- delete it anytime from Settings. Source code viewable at github.com/freshcod3s/cta-app.

Not affiliated with the US government. Not investment advice. Disclosures may be delayed or amended. Verify at disclosures-clerk.house.gov and ethics.senate.gov. Free. No ads. No tracking.
